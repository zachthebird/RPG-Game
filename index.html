Pog Prowess!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four pogs. By clicking on a pog, you will add a certain number of pog-points to your score.

You win the game by matching your pog-points to the random number provided.

The number of pog-points alotted to each pog varies from pog to pog and is not displayed, you'll have to figure out how many pog-points each pog is worth by clicking the pog.

Each time you start a new game, the pog-points will vary.

Good luck, and may the strongest pogger win!
